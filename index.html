Hello There!

My name is Christopher Akinbile. I'm a professor of Agricultural Engineering at the Federal University of Technology, Akure.

University Education, with dates

  • Undergraduate: The Federal University of Technology, Akure. (1992 - 1999)
  • Postgraduate: University of Ibadan, Oyo state. (2000 - 2001)
  • Postgraduate: University of Ibadan, Oyo state. (2004 - 2009)

Academic Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Engineering (B.Eng) degree, January 1999, (Project :Design and Testing of a simple Lysimeter for the tropics)
  • Master of Science (M.Sc) degree, December 2001, (Thesis: Determination of Consumptive Water Use of Jute Mallow (Corchorus olitorius L.) using Blaney-Morin Simulation Model)
  • Doctor of Philosophy (ph.D) degree, March 2009, (Thesis : Upland Rice yield Responses to Differential Water Application under Sprinkler Irrigation System).

I was among the 100 world's best researchers on climate change in a forum founded by Volkswagen Foundation, Germany in November 2010. I also won my institution's (FUTA) Vice-Chancellor's award for remarkable achievement due to my outstanding contribution to the development of the university in November 2012.

I was elected by Moszicque Ezine Magazine based in New Delhi, India as one of the future world young leaders. I am married to Pharm (Mrs) Olukemi Akinbile, who is a pharmacist at the University's health centre, and our marriage is blessed with 3 wonderful children.
